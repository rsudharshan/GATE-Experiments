Sheraton Interested in a change from the Royal Viking, I decided to try the Sheraton on a recent trip to Stockholm. It is a bit further from the Arlanda Express train station. While I was able to walk, others might find it a bit far. Unfortunately, it's also too close for many taxi drivers to be bothered. The gym was small and didn't have a single treadmill! Unique Quality: Rooms are good sized and the breakfast is quite good. The lobby is nice with a welcoming fireplace and frequent live music, but I was disappointed that the wine bar never seemed to be open. Opinion of Price: more expensive than average
