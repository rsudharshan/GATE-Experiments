Very expensive, very average !!!!! The Stockholm Sheraton, considered one of the best hotels in all of Sweden. I found the place to be very average compared to other 5 star hotels around the world. The staff is friendly and speak 30 different languages, but the rooms have nothing special and no 1 thing that makes this hotel stand out, it does have a great location facing the harbour which of course is $50 US more a night. But other than location, I found it to be very average and very expensive....a night runs about $350 a night plus another 17% in tax....very expensive in an already very expensive city..... 5 out 10. Opinion of Price: most expensive
