Could be much better I went to Sweden with two other friends and somebody decided to stay in the Sheraton. Taking in consideration that the price we were offered for a suite was around 240Eur we decided to take one of those, the girls would stay in the bedroom and I would be happy enough with the sofa bed in the sitting room. Well, the room was very small and it wasn't even prepared with a 3rd bed. There were a few other minor problems, but for that price you're expecting a bit more.
